Welcome to my page about the FIFA World Cup 2026

This year, 48 countries face off to find a world champion. Over 39 days, teams first face off in the group stage where they compete to earn points in their bracket. At the end of the group stage, the top 2 teams in each group qualify for the knockout rounds along with the top 8 3rd place teams. The knockout stage is a single elimination styled bracket, meaning one loss, and the team is eliminated from the tournament. Matches take place throughout North America, featuring arenas in Mexico, the United States, and Canada.
